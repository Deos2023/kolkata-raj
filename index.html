Our Story

How We Started

When I was a driver, my income was limited and I couldn't afford what I wanted in life. I discussed with my friends and family about starting something new, and they encouraged me to open this kind of business. With their support and my hard work, I started Kolkata Raj Self Drive Car Rental in 2021.

Today, we proudly serve customers all over Kolkata, helping them rent cars easily and affordably. We are committed to providing reliable, clean, and well-maintained cars for rent. Anyone can rent a car from us for any occasion, anywhere in Kolkata.
